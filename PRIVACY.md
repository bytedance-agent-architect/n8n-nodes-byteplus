# Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

## Data Collection
This application collects:
- TikTok account information (username, open ID) via OAuth
- Videos you choose to upload

## Data Usage
Your data is used solely to:
- Authenticate with TikTok on your behalf
- Upload videos you specify to your TikTok account

## Data Storage
- We do not store your videos
- OAuth tokens are stored locally in your n8n instance

## Third Party Services
This application uses the TikTok Content Posting API. Your use is also subject to TikTok's Privacy Policy.

## Contact
For questions, open an issue on this repository.
